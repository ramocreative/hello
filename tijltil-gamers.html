TiJLTiL Gamers

Brand Identity - Saudi Arabia | 2022

TiJLTiL Gamers is a Saudi content creator on the YouTube platform, who posts educational clips on how to obtain or win special prizes for games on the PlayStation.

The addition of the Trophy shape is evidence of achievement and challenge, as the channel explains the challenges of the games and how to accomplish them. As for the shape of the Controller, it indicates games and entertainment, which is the main content in the channel.
