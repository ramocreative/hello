Dupamicaffeine

Brand Identity - Saudi Arabia | 2023

Dupamicaffeine is a YouTube channel owned by Nasser Al-Aqeel, a content creator born in 1993 in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. He has a YouTube channel where he publishes content related to books. He also has a channel on Twitch, in which he publishes live broadcasts of electronic games.

The challenge was to develop an association that included both the main brand (the channel for publishing content for books) and the other sub-brand (the channel for publishing content for games). The goal was to create a look that had a extroverted and flexible personality.

In order to create something that meets all the needs of both brands, I chose a flexible color structure according to each brand and logo derived from the main brand to maintain the identity and character of the original brand.
