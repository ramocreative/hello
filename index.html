About me

Who am I ?

Hi! I'm Raman, known as Ramo Creative. A self-taught graphic designer from Iraq, specializing in logo design and brand identity.br

I am an experienced logo and branding designer with over 5 years of expertise in translating client visions into captivating visual identities. With a deep understanding of the power of branding, I strive to create designs that leave a lasting impression.

Known for my innovative approach, I have a proven track record of delivering designs that resonate with target audiences and help businesses stand out from the competition. My portfolio showcases a diverse range of successful projects, demonstrating my versatility and adaptability to various industries and design aesthetics.

Beyond logo and branding design, I also have a strong foundation in graphic design principles. I excel in creating cohesive brand assets across multiple mediums, including packaging design, social media graphics, and other marketing collateral. By leveraging my expertise in these areas, I provide comprehensive visual solutions that strengthen brand consistency and recognition.

Customer satisfaction is my top priority, and I pride myself on delivering exceptional customer service. I value effective communication and collaboration, ensuring I fully understand client needs and objectives. By incorporating client feedback throughout the design process, I create designs that not only meet expectations but exceed them.

I am passionate about staying up-to-date with industry trends, regularly seeking inspiration and refining my skills to offer cutting-edge designs. My commitment to professional growth and dedication to providing high-quality designs make me a valuable asset to any branding project.

Overall, I bring a unique blend of creativity, technical expertise, and a client-focused approach to each project. My goal is to empower businesses to elevate their brands, establish a strong brand presence, and make a lasting impact in today's competitive marketplace.
