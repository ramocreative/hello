iMAD

Brand Identity - Iraq | 2023

Hussein Al-Jazairi runs iMAD Tech platforms on social media, produces high-quality videos primarily around tech, cars or other topics to an audience over 5M total followers on iMAD Tech Platforms. win special prizes for games on the PlayStation.

The challenge was to develop an association that represented the content being offered (device and car reviews). The goal was to create an identity that was both attractive and flexible.

In order to create something that caters to all branding needs, I chose a flexible color structure as well as a brand-appropriate font family.
